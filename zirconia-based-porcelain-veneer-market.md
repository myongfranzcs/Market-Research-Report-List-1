<p><strong>The Zirconia Based Porcelain Veneer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No Layering Porcelain</li><li>Layering With Porcelain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zirconia-based porcelain veneer market is segmented into two main types: no layering porcelain and layering with porcelain. No layering porcelain involves using a solid block of zirconia that is milled to create a strong, durable veneer without additional layers, providing a uniform appearance and reduced risk of chipping. In contrast, layering with porcelain combines a zirconia base with multiple layers of porcelain to enhance aesthetics, allowing for better color variations and translucency, but may be more prone to wear over time.</p></p>
