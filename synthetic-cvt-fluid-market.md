<p><p>The synthetic CVT fluid market is divided into two main types: fully synthetic oil and semi-synthetic oil. Fully synthetic oil is formulated entirely from synthetic base stocks, offering superior performance, stability, and protection under extreme conditions, making it ideal for high-performance vehicles. On the other hand, semi-synthetic oil combines synthetic and conventional oils, providing a balanced option that offers improved performance compared to traditional fluids while being more cost-effective. Both types cater to varying consumer needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
