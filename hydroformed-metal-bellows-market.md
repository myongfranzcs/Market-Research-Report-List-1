<p><p>The hydroformed metal bellows market comprises two main types: single wall and multi-wall bellows. Single wall bellows are characterized by a single layer of metal, offering flexibility and simplicity in design, typically used for applications requiring moderate pressure and movement control. Conversely, multi-wall bellows consist of multiple concentric layers, providing enhanced durability, pressure resistance, and thermal insulation. These are commonly employed in more demanding environments, such as aerospace and industrial applications, where robust performance is critical.</p></p>
