<p><ul><li>Mineral</li><li>Chemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete admixture construction chemicals market is divided into two primary types: mineral admixtures and chemical admixtures. Mineral admixtures, such as fly ash, silica fume, and slag, enhance the durability and strength of concrete by improving its workability and reducing permeability. Chemical admixtures include superplasticizers, retarders, and accelerators, which modify the properties of fresh concrete to enhance flow, extend setting times, or speed up curing. Both types significantly contribute to achieving desired performance characteristics in concrete construction.</p></p>
