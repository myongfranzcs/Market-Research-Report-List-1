<p><strong>The Thermosyphon Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Thermosiphon</li><li>Water-Cooled Thermosiphon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermosyphon cooling system market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled thermosiphons and water-cooled thermosiphons. Air-cooled thermosiphons utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for applications where water is scarce. They are often used in electronics cooling. Conversely, water-cooled thermosiphons rely on water as the cooling medium, providing efficient heat transfer and better cooling performance, ideal for industrial operations or high-performance systems. These variations cater to diverse applications based on cooling requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
