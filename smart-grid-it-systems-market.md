<p><ul><li>Distributed Energy Resource Management System (DERMS)</li><li>Demand Response Management System (DRMS)</li><li>Geographic Information System (GIS)</li><li>Customer Information System (CIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Grid IT systems encompass various market types crucial for modern energy management. Distributed Energy Resource Management Systems (DERMS) optimize the integration and operation of distributed energy resources. Demand Response Management Systems (DRMS) facilitate consumer participation in energy conservation during peak periods. Geographic Information Systems (GIS) enhance the visualization and analysis of electric grid assets and spatial data. Customer Information Systems (CIS) manage customer interactions, billing, and service records, improving customer engagement and operational efficiency within the energy sector.</p></p>
