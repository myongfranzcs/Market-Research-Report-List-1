<p><p>The HDPE conduit pipes market is primarily divided into two types: flexible and rigid. In contrast, rigid HDPE conduit pipes offer enhanced structural support and protection for electrical and communication cables in fixed installations. Both types cater to different application needs, with flexible options favored in dynamic environments and rigid options used in more static settings.</p></p>
