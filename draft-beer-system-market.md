<p><strong>The Draft Beer System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Draw</li><li>Long-Draw</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Draft Beer System market comprises three main types: Direct-Draw, Long-Draw, and Air Cooled. Direct-Draw systems serve beer directly from the keg, ideal for short distances, ensuring freshness and minimal equipment. Long-Draw systems are used for longer distances, employing insulated lines and gas pressure to maintain beer quality over extended runs. Air Cooled systems utilize chilled air to keep the beer cool as it travels, suitable for various bar layouts. Each system caters to different operational needs and space configurations.</p></p>
