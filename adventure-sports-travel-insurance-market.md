<p><p>Adventure Sports Travel Insurance comes in two primary market types: Personal Insurance and Group Insurance. Personal Insurance caters to individual travelers, providing tailored coverage for solo adventurers engaging in activities like skiing, rock climbing, or scuba diving. It ensures protection against unexpected accidents, injuries, or cancellations. Group Insurance, on the other hand, is designed for organized trips involving multiple participants, like family vacations or corporate retreats. It offers combined coverage, often at a reduced rate per person, while addressing collective risks inherent in group activities.</p></p>
