<p><p>The aramid market is primarily segmented into para-aramid and meta-aramid fibers. Para-aramid fibers, known for their high tensile strength and thermal stability, are commonly used in applications such as aerospace, automotive, and personal protective equipment. In contrast, meta-aramid fibers are characterized by their excellent heat and flame resistance, making them ideal for applications in electrical insulation, firefighting gear, and industrial fabrics. Both types serve critical roles in industries requiring advanced materials for durability and safety.</p></p>
