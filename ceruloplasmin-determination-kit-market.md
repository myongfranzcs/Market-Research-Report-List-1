<p><strong>The Ceruloplasmin Determination Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nephelometry</li><li>Immunoturbidimetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceruloplasmin Determination Kit market includes analytical methods such as nephelometry and immunoturbidimetry, which are utilized for measuring ceruloplasmin levels in serum. Nephelometry involves detecting light scattered by antigen-antibody complexes, providing quantitative results based on particle concentration. Immunoturbidimetry, on the other hand, measures the cloudiness of a sample when an antigen-antibody reaction occurs, indicating the concentration of ceruloplasmin. Both methods are valued for their sensitivity and specificity in diagnosing conditions related to copper metabolism and associated disorders.</p></p>
