<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Construction Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"NRB"</li><li>"LRB"</li><li>"HDR"</li><li>"Others"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction isolator market comprises various types used to reduce vibration and seismic impacts on structures. "NRB" (Non-Restaining Bearings) provide flexibility without limiting movement, making them ideal for horizontal loads. "LRB" (Lead Rubber Bearings) combine elastomeric properties with lead cores, enhancing energy dissipation during seismic events. "HDR" (High-Damping Rubber) bearings offer superior damping capabilities, effectively managing oscillations and vibrations. "Others" encompass alternative solutions, including sliding bearings and custom-designed systems, tailored for specific structural needs and performance criteria in construction projects.</p></p>
