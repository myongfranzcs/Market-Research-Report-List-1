<p><p>The BBQ grill charcoal market features several types, including lump charcoal, which consists of raw wood processed into chunks, offering high heat and a natural flavor. Charcoal briquettes are compressed and uniform, providing longer burn times and consistent heat. Extruded charcoal, formed through a molding process, achieves a denser structure for efficient burning. Sugar charcoal, made from sugarcane waste, is eco-friendly and provides a unique flavor profile. Each type caters to varying grilling preferences and users' needs.</p></p>
