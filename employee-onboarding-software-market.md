<p><p>Employee onboarding software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based onboarding solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from any location, which suits organizations of varying sizes. In contrast, on-premises software is installed directly onto a company's own servers, providing enhanced security and control over data. While on-premises solutions can be more costly and require IT maintenance, they are preferred by organizations with strict data compliance needs.</p></p>
