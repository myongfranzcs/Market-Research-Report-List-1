<p><strong>The Over-the-horizon Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skywave Over-the-horizon Radar</li><li>Groundwave Over-the-horizon Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Over-the-horizon radar (OTHR) technology is typically categorized into two main types: Skywave and Groundwave OTHR. Skywave OTHR utilizes ionospheric reflection to detect targets at long distances, making it effective for tracking aircraft and missile activities beyond the radar’s line of sight. In contrast, Groundwave OTHR relies on radar signals that travel along the Earth's surface, suitable for detecting maritime activities and ground-based threats. Both types play crucial roles in military surveillance and national defense strategies.</p></p>
