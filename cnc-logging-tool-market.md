<p><ul><li>Open Hole Logging</li><li>Cased Hole Logging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC logging tools are essential in the oil and gas industry for determining the properties of subsurface formations. Open hole logging involves measuring geological formations through uncased boreholes, providing valuable data on porosity, permeability, and fluid content. Cased hole logging, on the other hand, analyzes formations within cased wells, focusing on the evaluation of well integrity and production optimization. Both applications enhance decision-making for exploration and production, driving efficiency and maximizing resource extraction in various geological settings.</p></p>
