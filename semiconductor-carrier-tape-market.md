Paper core carrier tape is lightweight, eco-friendly, and typically used for cost-sensitive applications. It offers adequate protection for semiconductor components during transportation and storage. In contrast, plastic core carrier tape is more durable and moisture-resistant, making it suitable for high-value, sensitive electronic components.
