<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasound Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental ultrasound units market comprises primarily two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric devices. Magnetostrictive units utilize a magnetic field to generate vibrations in the instrument's tip, effectively disrupting calculus and biofilm during dental procedures. In contrast, piezoelectric devices leverage crystal oscillation for precise ultrasonic vibrations, offering improved control and reduced heating during operations. Both technologies enhance efficiency and effectiveness in dental treatments, catering to varying practitioner preferences and clinical requirements within the dental industry.</p></p>
